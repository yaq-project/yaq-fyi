Projects we learned about ¶

bluesky ¶

  • Collection of sub projects
  • ophyd for hardware abstraction
  • bluesky for acquisition orchestration
  • suitcase for data recording/serialization
  • databroker for data management
  • Runs large scale facilities (including NSLS-II
  • Explicitly designed to scale down to single PI labs

Mjolnir ¶

  • "Scientific IOT" sensor management
  • SciPy Talk

1000x Lab ¶

  • Enthought initiative for open hardware and lab automation
  • Includes a "Congress" (think small conference)
  • SciPy Talk

yaqc-bluesky ¶

After attending SciPy 2020 and talking to the developers of Bluesky, we at yaq have decided to push forward with a first class bridge from yaq into bluesky. This will enable users of yaq to tap into the powerful data acquisition pipeline provided by Bluesky.

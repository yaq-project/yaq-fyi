yaq/contact

We would love to hear from you! It's typically best to start by opening an issue on yaq-fyi. We treat this issue tracker as a general forum, so please do not hesitate to open an issue for any reason! We will redirect you if necessary.

To subscribe to our listserve, send an email to yaq+subscribe@g-groups.wisc.edu

A full list of contact options follows:

yaq-fyi issue tracker ("forum")
#yaq:matrix.org (chat)
yaq@g-groups.wisc.edu (listserve archive)

Thank you for your interest in the yaq project.
